Hello!

My research focuses on the gaseous contents of galaxies and their evolution. I utilize emission and absorption spectra of galaxies to quantify the dynamic properties of galaxies through the use of both observed and simulated data. I received my Bachelor of Science with a double-major in Physics: Comprehensive Physics and Astronomy from the University of Washington in Seattle in the spring of 2022. Currently, I'm a graduate student at the University of Chicago's Department of Astronomy & Astrophysics.
