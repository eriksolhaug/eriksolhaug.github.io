Hello!

My research focuses on the gaseous contents of galaxies and their evolution. I utilize emission and absorption spectra of galaxies to quantify the dynamic properties of galaxies through the use of both observed and simulated data. I received my Bachelor of Science with a double-major in Physics: Comprehensive Physics and Astronomy from the University of Washington in Seattle in the spring of 2022. At the UW, I analyzed absorption spectra from the COS spectrograph on the Hubble Space Telescope and used numerical photoionization code to predict the emitted spectra from some of the most diffuse gas in the universe. I am currently a graduate student at the University of Chicago's Department of Astronomy & Astrophysics.
